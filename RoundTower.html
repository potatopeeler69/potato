The Round Tower (Rundetårn)

The Round Tower (aka. Rundetårn) is a place of wonders, a big tower with an amazing view from the top and a mini museum inside is the perfect place to spend a couple of hours in and have a good time.

Adresse: Købmagergade 52A, 1150 København

Website: rundetårn.dk
