Rosenborg

The Rosenborg castle is the perfect combination of a buitiful park and a nice museum. The park is a big place where you can have a nice picnic or maybe see the the ducks flapping around in the canal, as for the castle itself it is a nice glance into the items of the royal family with an underground safe that holds the queen's crown.

Adresse: Øster Voldgade 4A, 1350 København
