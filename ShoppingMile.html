Strøget Shopping mile

Strøget the shopping mile that is located just next to Kongens Nytorv metro station and is the perfect place for you to part with your money as the many shops will be sure to part you with your money. All jokes aside it is a nice place for you to have a nice long walk relax and maybe even get yourself a nice new suit or dress while you're at it.
