National Museum of Denmark

The national museum of denmark is just like any other museum except more fun and interesting for the smaller children that can play on the many various styled playgrounds. But if you aren't into that kind of stuff you can always go into the "museum" part of the museum where there are many different exponates, ranging from viking tools and weapons to the way that vikings built coffins out of tree logs.
